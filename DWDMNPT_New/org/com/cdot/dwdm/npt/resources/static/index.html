About Us

Network Planning Tool is a user-friendly network planner for optimized deployement of DWDM Systems.

NPT adds intelligence and automation to DWDM optical network by providing a comprehensive set of engineering, planning and analysis functions for DWDM networks. With a highly user-friendly GUI , NPT makes network planning a piece of cake even with most complex of configurations .

Features

NPT provides you with a wide variety of planning features, thus helping you in deploying an optimised and cost-effective network.

Map Creation

Create your own map using our interactive drawing board with functionalities like drag&drop , mouse/keyboard interaction and a handful of more events to support your map creation task.

Circuit Processing

After you create your map , you can generate traffic by giving circuits as input to RWA algorithm. You can set protection type , path type , protection mechanism ,etc using circuits tab.

RWA Algorithm

Routing and Wavelength Assignment algorithm feeds our planning tool with optimized processing and thus helps you in generating the most efficient network.

Fault Analysis

After you have network routes , you can analyze your network for faults with our interactive fault analysis feature. You can generate faults on nodes and links .

Manage/View Reports

NPT has different views for detailed tabular information . You can easily download pdf versions of Bill of Material , Inventory , Optical Data,etc.

Brown Field

After you deploy the network , you can reconfigure your network using NPT for optimized changes in case of addition ,removal or modification of nodes/links.
